# Meet me alpha success metrics

## Core product
- signup completion
- joined group in first session
- first post or first message in first session
- invite creation rate
- invite acceptance rate

## Operator metrics
- unresolved bug count
- feedback volume by category
- open moderation reports
- activation score from launch dashboard

## Healthy signs
- testers come back after day 1
- users join and talk in groups
- creators generate invites
- feedback trends are understandable and actionable
