# Meet me closed alpha plan

## Batch 1
- 20 highly engaged testers
- focus on onboarding, posting, joining groups, and invite sharing

## Batch 2
- 50 broader testers
- focus on retention, creator analytics, moderation patterns, and referral conversion

## Daily operator routine
- check /status
- check /launch-dashboard
- check /creator
- review moderation and unread notifications
- log top friction points

## Exit criteria for alpha
- stable auth and content flows
- acceptable invite conversion
- no major moderation blind spots
- monitoring and deployment confidence
