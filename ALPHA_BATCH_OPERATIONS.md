# Meet me alpha batch operations

## Batch 1 target
- 5 to 10 testers

## Batch 1 goals
- validate first-session activation
- validate group join and first post
- validate chat usefulness
- validate invite creation and acceptance
- collect top UX confusion themes

## Daily operator checklist
- review /status
- review /qa
- review /launch-dashboard
- review /admin/feedback
- review /admin/feedback-analytics

## Decision to expand batch
Expand only if:
- no critical auth failures
- no blocking database or RLS failures
- feedback queue is manageable
- moderation issues are limited
