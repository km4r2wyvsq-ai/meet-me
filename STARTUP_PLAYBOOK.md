# Startup-level product direction for Meet me

## Positioning
Meet me is a public, text-first community platform where people discover groups through interests and stay for repeat social rituals.

## Growth systems now present in the demo
- notification center for retention loops
- moderation queue for safety operations
- founder metrics for activation visibility
- role-based demo access for member, moderator, and admin
- event stream to clarify what should later become analytics and audit logs

## What to build next for a production launch
- server-side roles and permissions using Supabase auth claims or membership tables
- real analytics provider integration
- email and push notifications
- moderation history and admin notes
- ranking, search, and recommendations
- monetization experiments later

## Suggested launch strategy
Start with three wedge communities instead of everyone:
- creators building personal brands
- city and local interest communities
- founder and freelancer groups

## Key metrics
- signup to join-group conversion
- joined-group to first-post conversion
- seven-day retained users
- messages per active group
- percent of groups with weekly active posts
